Conducted groundbreaking research under the guidance of UTD Professor Dr. Octavious Smiley, translating the PANDA algorithm (originally used for gene network reconstruction) into the financial domain to develop a pairs trading strategy, achieving cumulative returns of 95% over an 8-year period during backtesting. I've personally developed the Copula pairs trading algorithm to backtest both the baseline and PANDA Algorithm(not on github as this was done on quant connect). Checkout GitHub here
